Nali was born in Khaku-Khol, a village belongs to Sharazur in Sulaimany,
Kurdistan region of Iraq. As was the custom in the old days in Kurdistan,
he started studying the Quran first and Arabic language in mosques in Kurdistan,
then he became a Faqi. “Faqi" is a Mullah’s student, which is the name of students in mosques.
